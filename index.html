Here's why cats are great. Cats have a unique kind of cuteness. They show affection through their large green eyes, they depict playfulness by chasing laser lights and toy spiders, and they become a part of your home. Whether cats want you to buzz off, entertain them, give them food, or show them TV, they have a specific way of meowing for each activity. A slow, lulled meow signals that they want you to pet them under their necks. A short purr lets you know that they are impressed. A loud, continuous meow is their way of asking you to "get the hell out of here!" It is difficult to think of anything cuter than this.
